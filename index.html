About Me

Elaine is an Industrial Engineer who specialised in logistic engineering and supply chain management. She has industry experience in inventory management, supply chain planning, procurement and process engineering.

Her desire to optimise the supply chain processes and improve its ease of access through programming led her to become a Full Stack Web Developer.

She spends her leisure time doing computer-aided design, photography, gaming, and biking.
